What is World of Warcraft?

World of Warcraft is an MMORPG(Massive Multiplayer Online RolePlaying Game) Where you can take on the role as a young adventure on two very different sides of a war between the Alliance and the Horde.

On the left, you have the Alliance, under the guidance of King Varian Wrynn, the Alliance believes in honor, valor and to enstill order across Azeroth. On the right, you have the Horde, under the leadership of Warchief Vol'jin, the Horde all share the same values of Honor, Dignity and Freedom.
